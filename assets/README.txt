DROP YOUR OWN SCENE IMAGES HERE
================================

Put image files in this folder with these exact names to replace the
built-in drawings. If a file is missing, the app falls back to the drawn art.

  colossal.png   -> shows when you log a meal over 1,500 kcal
  wall.png       -> shows when you hit a streak milestone (Wall Maria/Rose/etc.)
  wings.png      -> shows for "Freedom" and when you triple-tap the title

Tips for the best look:
- Transparent-background PNG works best (the image floats on the dark overlay
  instead of showing a rectangular box). Search "colossal titan png transparent".
- Animated GIFs also work and will actually MOVE. Name it colossal.gif and
  change the name in app.js (SCENE_IMG) from colossal.png to colossal.gif,
  or just tell Claude and it'll switch it.
- Roughly square, at least 500x500px, looks sharpest.
- These are for your personal use on your own phone.

You can also add more scenes later (rumbling.png, levi.png, etc.) — just ask.
